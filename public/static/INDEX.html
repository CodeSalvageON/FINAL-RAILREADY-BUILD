The Shooting Range
 
              

                
The Tec-9

                
                
The Tec-9 was used by Sweet-T and G.O. in the Central City School Shooting, and was later used by WiiU Owner in the Palm Tree Party. It is a pistol prone to jamming. The gun in action.
 
                
The MP-40

                
                
The MP-40 was used by Sweet-T against three Curriculum Associates security guards. The German submachine gun is iconic, and usually fully automatic, but strangely the one Sweet-T used was semi-automatic only. The gun in action.
 
                
The X26 Taser

                
                
The X26 Taser was used primarily by Curriculum Associates Security Guards, but they proved to be useless both in terms of range and in damage against Sweet-T's MP-40. The gun in action.

                
The PLR-16

                
                
The PLR-16 was a pistol chambered in .223 used by Jake The Jihader during the Palm Tree Party. It was left behind by WiiU Owner, who favored the Tec-9. It is rumored to be more reliable and just as accurate as an AR-15. The gun in action.

                
The FAD

                
                
The FAD was an assault rifle used by Jake The Jihader. Jake used the FAD to escape from a crashed school bus, and would later use it to kill a security guard and a school board member. No videos of this gun ;(

                
The Kel Tec Sub-2000

                
                
The Kel Tec Sub-2000 was a handy 9mm carbine used by Plory during the Daycare Center Massacre. It had a foldable stock and could be concealed easily. The gun in action.

                
The Claridge Hi Tec S9

                
                
The Claridge Hi Tec S9 was a semi-automatic pistol used by Yoop during the Daycare Center Massacre. Yoop had modified his pistol so that it slam-fired, making it fully automatic. The gun in action.
